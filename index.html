Western Wedding

Where shall I begin? February 15th, 2017 isn't only the day we got married, but the moment our families met for the first time, and probably the only time in our lives where almost all of our closest friends gather in one place to celebrate this special moment with us. When we decided to get married in India never ever had we thought so many of you would make it all the way to Bangalore, and yet here we were! This was special because you made it special for us and we will remember this day for the rest of our lives.
